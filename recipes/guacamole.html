Guacamole

Description

Use this recipe to make delicious guacamole!

It's really easy and very filling. We recommend eating with Doritos.

Ingredients

  • 2 avocados
  • Onion
  • Tomato
  • 1/2 lime (squeezed)

Steps

  1. Mash the avocado.
  2. Chop the onion and tomato finely.
  3. Add lime juice.
  4. Mix thoroughly.
  5. Chill in fridge.
